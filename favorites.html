Favorite Streaming Series

The Big Bang Theory
The Big Bang Theory is an american sitcom that started in 2007, centered on five characters living in Pasadena, California: Leonard Hofstadter and Sheldon Cooper, both physicists at Caltech, who share an apartment; Penny, a waitress and aspiring actress who lives across the hall who Leonard had a huge crush on; and Leonard and Sheldon's coworkers who were just as equally nerdy, aerospace engineer Howard Wolowitz who also at the begining had a major crush on penny and would compete with Leonard and astrophysicist Raj Koothrappali who had and extreme anxiety of talking to women while sober but once he had a drink he was the biggest player out of all of them. This series is no longer in production but is by far my most favorite streaming series.

Young Sheldon
Is an extension of The Big Bang Theory it gives a little more back story on how sheldon cooper came to be, in Young Sheldon he is a child prodigy who is very good at mathematic and Science, Sheldon knows that he is a child prodigy and often shows his superior intellect to feel more dominate over people he meets or try to down play his intellect. Young sheldon lacks social ques which causes his superior intellect to come off as rude or obnoxious, the show gives the back story of the difficulty that he had with others and his family, sheldons dad often had issues giving him guidance because sheldon felt as if his family was stupid and didnt want to listen to someone with less intellect then him, as the show goes on the relationship between him and his family improve and shows the loving background he was raised in.

The Good Doctor
This show is about a autistic surgical resident named Shaun Murphy, as a kid his grew up in a very abusive house hold and ultimately him and his brother ran away from home, the two lived in a bus and would roam around and play with other kids around, shauns little brother steve acted as shauns older brother he would make sure shaun ate, he would even steal or obtain books to give to shaun so he can study and be successful, one day shaun and steve were playing with other kids at a railway and steve fell off of the train and ultimately dying Shaun was devistated and ran to a vetinarian that tried to help them in the past who became his mentor into becoming a surgical resident.

Arcane
Is a digital animated series that is based off of the lore of popular MOBA League of Legends, the show is about a cival war in a city called piltover where the rich lived on the top of the city and the poor lived underneath, the top would constantly opress the bottom and would exile any of the people from the bottom to reach the top it was heavily guarded, the two main character vi and jinx would steal from the top to be able to survive, after one bad mission jinx and vi were seperated and the rat overlord of the bottom saved jinx and injected her with a antidote that enchanced her powers and made her survive the rat overlord then turned jinx on her sister vi and created a fued between the two.

Avatar: The Last Airbender
This show is about a fued between tribes Fire, Water, Earth, and Air no one but the airbenders could wield all four, unfortunately the airbenders were "extinct" and only the four tribes were known the fire nation was the most powerful of the 4 and would force themselves to overtake other tribes katara and Sokka of the water tribe were scouting the fire tribe when they were found and chased them until they accidentally ran into Ang the last airbender frozen in a ball of ice eventually ang was free'd from the ice and supported their efforts on defending against the fire nation.
